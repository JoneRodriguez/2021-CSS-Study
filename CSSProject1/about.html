Album What It's About
CryBaby Her Crybaby album was about a little girl growing up in a broken abusive family and trying to navigate herself in a world with beauty standards, high expectations of her, while struggling with mental illness.
K-12 K-12 is a surreal dark comedy musical which follows Cry Baby as she enters, as she puts it, “the worst years of [her] life.” Faced with bullies, cruel teachers and patriarchal mind control, Cry Baby fights the system with her good friends and an angelic spirit guide by her side. The lyrics are not subtle in their storytelling. Despite the lyrics being on-the-nose, Melanie has no qualms tack-ling heavy subjects like sexual assault and eating disorders. Some standouts on the album are “Wheels On The Bus,” “The Principal,” “Nurse’s Office,” and “Orange Juice.”
After School EP According to Melanie, the EP follows the story elements that are present in her sophomore album, K-12; however, the songs are more personal to Melanie rather than relating to her character, Cry Baby.
